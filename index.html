About Me

I am a software developer living in Houston, Texas, though I am originally from a suburban town called West Chester, near Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Right now I'm working as a contractor for HostGator, a web hosting company that's been around since 2002. I like working there, and do most of my backend development using the Perl programming language.

I wasn't always a software developer or otherwise working on computers, though I've been interested in them since I was ten years old. I've been an administrative assistant, pizza delivery boy, dishwasher, cashier, and various other menial jobs prior to happening into a job in Information Technology. I much prefer working as a developer!

Lately I've been learning more full-stack development using JavaScript. You can find examples of my work (mostly front-end for now) on my portfolio page.
